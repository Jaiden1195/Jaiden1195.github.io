Our Aircraft

At NovaFlight Dynamics we take pride in our aircraft that is an accurate display of our cutting-edge research, innovations, and development. We manufacture aircraft for both defense and commercial sectors of aerospace, with many features that set our aircraft apart from other manufactureres. As the largest United States contractor, NovaFlight Dynamics is committed to being at the forefront of the future of aerospace engineering

F-28 Falcon

The F-28 Falcon is a cutting-edge, stealth air superiority fighter designed for superior maneuverability and advanced combat capabilities. It combines stealth technology with exceptional speed and agility, making it one of the most formidable aircraft in modern air warfare.

Specifications:

  • Length: 62 feet 1 inch (18.9 meters)
  • Wingspan: 44 feet 6 inches (13.6 meters)
  • Height: 16 feet 8 inches (5.1 meters)
  • Max Takeoff Weight: 83,500 pounds (37,900 kg)
  • Powerplant: Two NovaFlight Dynamic F119-PW-100 turbofans
  • Thrust: 35,000 pounds each with afterburner
  • Top Speed: Approximately Mach 2.25
  • Range: 1,839 miles (2,965 kilometers)
  • Ceiling: 65,000 feet (19,812 meters)

B-1 Raider

The B-1 Raider is a supersonic strategic bomber known for its versatility and advanced capabilities. Designed to penetrate enemy defenses, it combines high speed, long range, and a large payload capacity. The B-1 features variable-sweep wings that enhance maneuverability and reduce drag, making it effective in various mission profiles, from nuclear deterrence to conventional warfare.

Specifications:

  • Length: 168 feet 8 inches (51.8 meters)
  • Wingspan: 137 feet 0 inches (41.8 meters) with wings spread
  • Height: 40 feet 9 inches (12.4 meters)
  • Max Takeoff Weight: 477,000 pounds (216,000 kg)
  • Powerplant: Four NovaFlight Dynamic F101-GE-102 turbofan engines
  • Thrust: 30,000 pounds each with afterburner
  • Top Speed: Approximately Mach 1.25
  • Range: 6,000 miles (9,600 kilometers) unrefueled
  • Ceiling: 60,000 feet (18,300 meters)

T-52 Globemaster

The T-52 Globemaster is a large military transport aircraft designed for rapid strategic airlift of troops and cargo to main operating bases or forward operating bases around the world. It is capable of operating from short and austere airfields, making it highly versatile for a range of missions, including oversized cargo transport, medical evacuations, and humanitarian assistance.

Specifications:

  • Length: 174 feet 0 inches (53 meters)
  • Wingspan: 169 feet 10 inches (51.75 meters)
  • Height: 55 feet 1 inch (16.76 meters)
  • Maximum Takeoff Weight: 585,000 pounds (265,000 kg)
  • Cargo Capacity: Up to 170,900 pounds (77,519 kg)
  • Crew: 3 (pilot, co-pilot, loadmaster)
  • Maximum Speed: Approximately 450 knots (518 mph, 833 km/h)
  • Range: 2,400 nautical miles (4,400 km) with a full payload
