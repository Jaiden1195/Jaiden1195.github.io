About Us

Mission

NovaFlight Dynamics is an aerospace engineering corporation dedicated to innovating and redefining air travel. By advancing both defense and commercial aviation, we are committed to conducting cutting-edge research that propels the future of modern air travel.


History

Founded in 1936 in Dallas, Texas, NovaFlight began its journey by manufacturing engines and aircraft for the Allied Powers during World War II. Our most notable engine from that era was the 1723-55 Twin Hornet, which powered the NovaFlight Dynamics F6F Super Hornet.In the years following the war, NovaFlight Dynamics continued to innovate and has since grown into one of the leading aerospace engineering companies in the world. NovaFlight Dynamics signed a 20-year contract with the United States Department of Defense post-war, a partnership that continues to this day.Today, NovaFlight Dynamics remains at the forefront of aerospace technology, adapting to the evolving demands of the industry while maintaining our commitment to excellence and innovation.
